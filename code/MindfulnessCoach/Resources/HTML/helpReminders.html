This app can remind you to do exercises, be mindful at random times, and track your practice. As long as your phone is on you will be reminded according to the settings that you choose.

Schedule a Mindfulness Activity allows you to plan to do a specific activity at a future date and time of your choosing.

Set Random Reminders alerts you to be aware and mindful at unexpected times. Don't worry, you can set times when you should not be bothered.

Remind Me To Log will alert you to fill in your log so that you can track the mindfulness activities you are doing whether they are in this app or not.
