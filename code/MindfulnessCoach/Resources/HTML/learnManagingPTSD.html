Managing PTSD Symptoms During Mindfulness Practice

If you find you are triggered or overwhelmed with PTSD symptoms during mindfulness practice:

  • Recognize that there is no need to “muscle through.” Practice at a level that may feel challenging, but that isn’t overwhelming.
  • Shift your attention away from internal states like feelings, thoughts or sensations.
  • Focus on sounds, sights, or people around you.
  • Take some deep breaths and focus on the sensation of your feet on the floor to “ground” yourself.
